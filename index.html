Victor Coronil, also known as "VR", was born on December 22, 1992 in Buenos Aires, Argentina. From the age of 13, he ventured into the field of Rap and HipHop influenced by rappers such as Vico C, Residente, Daddy Yankee and Eminem. With a short career in the urban genre environment, he has several projects with the production company ARMAGGEDDON RECORDS.
